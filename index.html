Bard Early College Web Page Summer 2024

This Summer Scholars course introduces students to the fundamentals of web development. Through a hands-on approach, students will learn HTML5, CSS, graphic editing, multimedia embedding, and more, with the ultimate goal of creating their own portfolio page hosted on Replit and Github. Emphasis will be placed on practical application, creativity, and collaborative learning.
